Configure Software Z-Axis Endstops

Router limits help Maslow avoid potential damage from plunging too deep or retracting too far. Follow the steps below to set the z axis limits.

Step 1: Clear Z-Axis upper and lower limits

Press the button to clear previously set limits if moving beyond them is desired, otherwise skip to step 2

Step 2: Set Z-Axis Upper Limit

Move your sled to the extreme upper limit and press the button to save the new position.

Step 3: Z-Axis Lower Limit

Move your sled to the extreme lower limit and press the button to save the new position.
